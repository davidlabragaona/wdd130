The Raft of Us

The Raft of Us is your gateway to thrilling whitewater adventures in the natural landscapes of San Martin de los Andes. Nestled in Argentina's Patagonia region, we provide exhilarating experiences that connect guests with the untamed beauty of the Andes Mountains.

With certified guides prioritizing safety and excitement, we offer tailored trips for all skill levels. From family-friendly floats to adrenaline-pumping rapids, our welcoming staff ensures every guest feels comfortable and excited about their journey.

At The Raft of Us, we believe in the power of shared experiences. We foster camaraderie among guests and staff, creating lasting memories and friendships. Immerse yourself in the rapids, soak in the stunning scenery, and let us be your guides on this incredible whitewater journey. Join us for an adventure of a lifetime in San Martin de los Andes, where The Raft of Us blends thrilling outdoor experiences with the natural wonders of Patagonia.

History

The Raft of Us was founded as part of an assignment for the WDD130 course at Brigham Young University Idaho. What began as a visionary project soon transformed into a passion-driven endeavor. Inspired by the love for adventure and the desire to provide thrilling experiences, a group of creative individuals joined forces to establish The Raft of Us. Drawing upon their expertise in web development, design, and outdoor activities, they sought to create a unique whitewater rafting company.

Through extensive research, partnerships with local guides, and a deep understanding of safety protocols, The Raft of Us quickly gained recognition as a reputable provider of exhilarating whitewater adventures. They embraced the spirit of collaboration and created an inclusive atmosphere that welcomed individuals from all backgrounds, fostering lasting connections and friendships.
