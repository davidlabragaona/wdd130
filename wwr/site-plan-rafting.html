Overview

Purpose

The main purpose will be to increase brand awareness, reach a wider audience and attract new customers. By carefully designing the site's sections we can showcase its services, packages and prices as well as provide information on the locations and types of rapids available. The site will offer online booking and payment options. The business will also use social media platforms to engage with customers, share photos and videos and even interact with customers through feedback and reviews.

Audience

We identify the target audience as:

  • Age: 18-45
  • Gender: Male and Female
  • Income: Middle to Upper class
  • Education: College-educated or higher

The Water Rafting activities are specially designed for:

  • Adventure-seekers and outdoor enthusiasts
  • Individuals with a passion for nature and the outdoors
  • Travelers looking for unique and exciting experiences
  • Groups such as friends, families, and corporate teams looking for team-building activities

Scenario 1:
Q: What type of experience is required?
A: We offer various packages for different skill levels and also provide guidance and training for those who are new to water rafting.

Scenario 2:
Q: What type of gear is needed?
A: Our website provides detailed information on all the necessary gear required for water rafting. We also offer rental services for those who don't have their own equipment.

Scenario 3:
Q: Are there any age or health restrictions for water rafting?
A: Yes, there are age or health restrictions. The minimum age is 10 years old. Safety is a key aspect of our business. We strive to provide a safe while enjoyable experience.
